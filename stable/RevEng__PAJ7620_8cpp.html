Description: This driver class can recognize 9 gestures and output the result, including move up, move down, move left, move right, move forward, move backward, circle-clockwise, circle-anti (counter) clockwise, and wave. The driver also allows changing the sensor to 'cursor mode' where it tracks the closest object in view on an (X,Y) coordinate system.
